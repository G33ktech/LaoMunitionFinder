Support


The map on the left side of the home page is designed to allow the user to identify a geographical location that they wish to gather sorties information on. To move the map simply click and hold the mouse button and move the mouse. To zoom in and get a closer look at an area, click on the map once with the mouse button and roll the mouse scroll wheel forward. To zoom out on the map click on the map once and roll the mouse scroll wheel backwards. By clicking on a desired location on the map it will provide a longitude and latitude value.


To get bomb information for the chosen geographical location, click on the 'Show Bombs' button. This will bring up information on the right side map within a radius of XXX of the latitudinal and longitudinal position choosen on the left side map. These placemarkers will be coloured to illustrate the intensity of bombing in the area, i.e. Purple is Extreme, Red is High, Yellow is Moderate and Blue is Low.


Zoom functionality can again be used to get a closer look at the number of bombs in the chosen area. When you zoom into individual bomb locations and click on the placemarker you will see information in regard to the class of bomb dropped, the actual geographical location, the target and the observed damage. Where damage is unobserved, there is a possibility of an Unexploded Ordinance (UXO), which signifies a potentially dangerous area.


Click on "Clear All Markers" to clear the right side map. Bomb information will not be displayed until a geographical location has been chosen on the left side map.
